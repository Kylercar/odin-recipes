Biscuits and Gravy

Description

Very simple and easy recipe that you'll regret eating afterwards.

Ingredients

  • 16oz of jumbo buttermilk biscuits
  • 1 package of Jimmy Dean Original Hearty Pork Sausage Crumbles
  • 1/4 Cup Flower
  • 2 and 1/2 cups of milk
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Start biscuits according to package directions
  2. Meanwhile, start cooking your meat in a skill over medium heat for 5-6 minutes or until thoroughly heated, stirring frequently.
  3. Stir in your flower, slowly add milk, cook until mixture comes to a boil. Add salt and pepper to taste
